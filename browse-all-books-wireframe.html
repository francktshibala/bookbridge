What are the main themes in Romeo and Juliet?
🤖
The main themes in Romeo and Juliet include:

1. Love vs. Hate: The pure love between Romeo and Juliet contrasts with the bitter hatred between their families.

2. Fate and Free Will: The concept of "star-crossed lovers" suggests destiny, while their choices also drive the tragedy.

3. Youth vs. Age: The impulsive passion of youth clashes with the rigid traditions of the older generation.

4. Death and Sacrifice: Their deaths ultimately reconcile the feuding families, showing love's power to overcome hatred.
