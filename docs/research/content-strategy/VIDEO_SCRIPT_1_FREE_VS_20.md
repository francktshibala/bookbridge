# Video Script #1: "FREE vs $20" Comparison

**Length:** 45 seconds  
**Concept:** FREE vs Competitor Comparison  
**Viral Potential:** 10/10  
**Difficulty:** Easy (screen recording)

---

## Complete Script with Timing

### [0-3 seconds] HOOK

**Text Overlay (Large, Bold, White on Dark Background):**
```
"Books too complex for ESL learners?"
```

**Visual:**
- Show complex book text (Pride & Prejudice original) or text overlay showing complex sentence
- Quick cut or text animation

**What to Say:**
```
"Classic books were written for native English speakers, not ESL learners. They're too complex to read."
```

**Voice Tone:** Understanding, relatable

---

### [3-10 seconds] PROBLEM

**Text Overlay:**
```
"Books written for native speakers, not ESL learners"
```

**Visual:**
- Show original Pride & Prejudice text (complex, long sentences)
- Or show text overlay: "Too complex for ESL learners"
- Point out complexity (long sentences, difficult words)

**What to Say:**
```
"Most classic books were written for American or English speakers. They use complex vocabulary and long sentences. ESL learners struggle to understand them. That's the problem we're solving."
```

**Voice Tone:** Empathetic, understanding the struggle

---

### [10-30 seconds] SOLUTION

**Text Overlay:**
```
"BookBridge: 100+ Books FREE"
```

**Visual (Screen Recording of BookBridge App):**
1. **[10-15s]** Show BookBridge homepage or book library
   - Scroll through books (Pride & Prejudice, Romeo & Juliet, etc.)
   - Show "FREE" messaging

2. **[15-20s]** Show text simplification feature
   - Click on a book (e.g., "Pride & Prejudice")
   - Show original text (complex)
   - Switch to A1 level (show simplified text)
   - Point out: "See how it simplifies?"

3. **[20-25s]** Show multiple books
   - Scroll through book library
   - Show "100+ books" or count

4. **[25-30s]** Show FREE messaging
   - Point to "FREE" text or signup button
   - Show "No credit card required" if visible

**What to Say:**
```
"BookBridge simplifies ANY classic book to your exact ESL level. Watch: [pause while showing app] Here's Pride & Prejudice. See this complex text? [point to original] I can switch to A1 level [click level switch]. Now it's simple enough for beginners [point to simplified]. BookBridge simplifies books to 6 different levels - A1, A2, B1, B2, C1, C2. Pick your level. 100+ classic books, all FREE. No credit card required."
```

**Voice Tone:** Excited, showing value, clear and confident

---

### [30-40 seconds] PROOF

**Text Overlay:**
```
"Join 259 users reading FREE"
```

**Visual:**
- Show testimonial text overlay:
  - "Ana: 10/10 rating, read 42 minutes"
  - "Teacher Nolan: Best tool in 12 years"
- Or show app with user count

**What to Say:**
```
"Ana read for 42 minutes straight and gave it a 10 out of 10 rating. Teacher Nolan, with 12 years of experience, says it's the best tool he's seen. Join 259 users reading FREE books right now."
```

**Voice Tone:** Confident, social proof, trustworthy

---

### [40-45 seconds] CTA

**Text Overlay (Large, Prominent):**
```
"Try it FREE - Link in bio"
```

**Visual:**
- Show BookBridge signup button or URL
- Or show "bookbridge.app" text
- Arrow pointing to signup

**What to Say:**
```
"Try it FREE - link in bio. No credit card required. Start reading classic books today."
```

**Voice Tone:** Urgent but friendly, clear call to action

## Step-by-Step Recording Instructions

### Before Recording:
1. **Prepare App:**
   - Open BookBridge in browser
   - Go to featured books page
   - Have Pride & Prejudice ready to open
   - Test level switching (C2 → A1)

2. **Prepare Script:**
   - Read script out loud 2-3 times
   - Practice timing (45 seconds total)
   - Mark pauses in script

3. **Set Up Descript:**
   - Open Descript
   - New → Screen Recording
   - Select screen/browser window
   - Test audio (microphone working)

### During Recording:
1. **Start Recording:**
   - Click record in Descript
   - Wait 2 seconds (for editing buffer)

2. **Hook (0-3s):**
   - Say: "Classic books were written for native English speakers, not ESL learners. They're too complex to read."
   - Show complex book text or text overlay

3. **Problem (3-10s):**
   - Say: "Most classic books were written for American or English speakers. They use complex vocabulary and long sentences. ESL learners struggle to understand them. That's the problem we're solving."
   - Show original Pride & Prejudice text (complex, long sentences)

4. **Solution (10-30s):**
   - Say: "BookBridge simplifies ANY classic book to your exact ESL level. Watch:"
   - **[Pause]** Show book library (scroll through books)
   - Say: "Here's Pride & Prejudice."
   - Click on Pride & Prejudice
   - Say: "See this complex text? [point to original] I can switch to A1 level [click level switch]. Now it's simple enough for beginners [point to simplified]."
   - Show level selector (A1, A2, B1, B2, C1, C2)
   - Say: "BookBridge simplifies books to 6 different levels - A1, A2, B1, B2, C1, C2. Pick your level. 100+ classic books, all FREE. No credit card required."
   - Show FREE messaging

5. **Proof (30-40s):**
   - Say: "Ana read for 42 minutes straight and gave it a 10 out of 10 rating. Teacher Nolan, with 12 years of experience, says it's the best tool he's seen. Join 259 users reading FREE books right now."
   - Show testimonials or user count

6. **CTA (40-45s):**
   - Say: "Try it FREE - link in bio. No credit card required. Start reading classic books today."
   - Show signup button or URL

7. **Stop Recording:**
   - Wait 2 seconds after finishing
   - Stop recording

### After Recording:
1. **Review Recording:**
   - Watch full video
   - Check timing (should be ~45 seconds)
   - Note any mistakes

2. **Edit in Descript:**
   - Trim beginning/end (remove buffer time)
   - Add text overlays at timestamps
   - Add background music (low volume)
   - Export as MP4
