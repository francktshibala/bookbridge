# Video Creation Guide: How to Make Viral Videos That Get Users

**Purpose:** Step-by-step guide for creating videos that go viral and convert viewers into users  
**Tool:** Descript (you're already using it)  
**Goal:** Make videos that get 1,000+ views and convert 5-10% to signups

---

## 🎯 The Winning Formula

**Hook (First 3 seconds) → Problem → Solution → Proof → CTA**

**Length:** 30-60 seconds (sweet spot: 45 seconds)

**Why 45 seconds:**
- Your best video was 45 seconds (47 views)
- Long enough to show value
- Short enough to keep attention
- Perfect for YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels

---

## 📹 Video Concept #1: "FREE vs $20" Comparison (Highest Viral Potential)

**Why This Works:**
- Directly addresses cost pain point (biggest barrier)
- Creates "too good to be true" curiosity
- Positions BookBridge as better alternative
- Easy to create (screen recording)

**Video Structure:**

### Hook (0-3 seconds):
**Text Overlay:** "Paying $20 per simplified book?"
**Visual:** Show competitor pricing ($20/book)

**What to Say:** "Other apps charge $20 per simplified book. This is FREE."

### Problem (3-10 seconds):
**Text Overlay:** "ESL learners can't afford expensive books"
**Visual:** Show competitor pricing, expensive books

**What to Say:** "ESL learners struggle to find affordable reading materials. Graded readers cost $10-20 per book."

### Solution (10-30 seconds):
**Text Overlay:** "BookBridge: 100+ Books FREE"
**Visual:** Screen recording showing BookBridge app
- Show book library (100+ books)
- Show text simplification (A1-C2 levels)
- Show it's FREE

**What to Say:** "BookBridge makes ANY classic book FREE and simplified to your exact level. Watch: [show app demo]"

### Proof (30-40 seconds):
**Text Overlay:** "Join 259 users reading FREE"
**Visual:** Show testimonials (Ana, Nolan)

**What to Say:** "Ana read for 42 minutes straight. Teacher Nolan says it's the best tool he's seen in 12 years."

### CTA (40-45 seconds):
**Text Overlay:** "Try it FREE - Link in bio"
**Visual:** Show app URL or signup button

**What to Say:** "Try it FREE - link in bio. No credit card required."

**Total Length:** 45 seconds

## 🎬 How to Create Videos with Descript

### Step 1: Write Script (10 minutes)

**Use this template:**

```
HOOK (3 seconds):
[Text Overlay]: [Hook text]
[What to Say]: [Hook script]

PROBLEM (5 seconds):
[Text Overlay]: [Problem text]
[What to Say]: [Problem script]

SOLUTION (20 seconds):
[Text Overlay]: [Solution text]
[What to Say]: [Solution script]
[Visual]: [Screen recording of app]

PROOF (10 seconds):
[Text Overlay]: [Proof text]
[What to Say]: [Proof script]

CTA (5 seconds):
[Text Overlay]: [CTA text]
[What to Say]: [CTA script]
```

**Example Script (Video Concept #1):**

```
HOOK (3 seconds):
[Text Overlay]: "Paying $20 per simplified book?"
[What to Say]: "Other apps charge $20 per simplified book. This is FREE."

PROBLEM (5 seconds):
[Text Overlay]: "ESL learners can't afford expensive books"
[What to Say]: "ESL learners struggle to find affordable reading materials. Graded readers cost $10-20 per book."

SOLUTION (20 seconds):
[Text Overlay]: "BookBridge: 100+ Books FREE"
[What to Say]: "BookBridge makes ANY classic book FREE and simplified to your exact level. Watch: [show app demo]"
[Visual]: Screen recording showing BookBridge app - book library, text simplification

PROOF (10 seconds):
[Text Overlay]: "Join 259 users reading FREE"
[What to Say]: "Ana read for 42 minutes straight. Teacher Nolan says it's the best tool he's seen in 12 years."

CTA (5 seconds):
[Text Overlay]: "Try it FREE - Link in bio"
[What to Say]: "Try it FREE - link in bio. No credit card required."
```

### Step 2: Record Screen (5 minutes)

**Using Descript:**
1. Open Descript
2. Click "New" → "Screen Recording"
3. Record your screen showing BookBridge app
4. Follow script while recording
5. Keep it simple - one take is fine

**What to Record:**
- Book library (scroll through books)
- Text simplification (show level switching)
- App features (dictionary, audio sync)

**Tips:**
- Record in one take (you can edit later)
- Speak clearly and slowly
- Show app features naturally
- Don't worry about mistakes (edit in Descript)

### Step 3: Edit in Descript (30 minutes)

**Basic Editing:**
1. **Trim:** Cut beginning/end (keep 45 seconds total)
2. **Add Text Overlays:** Use Descript's text tool
   - Large, bold text
   - Contrasting colors (white text on dark background)
   - Place at top or bottom (not covering important content)
3. **Add Background Music:** Use Descript's music library
   - Upbeat, not distracting
   - Low volume (don't overpower voice)
4. **Export:** Export as MP4 (1080p, 30fps)

**Text Overlay Tips:**
- Use large font (readable on mobile)
- Bold, contrasting colors
- Keep text on screen 2-3 seconds
- Don't cover important app content

**Music Tips:**
- Use upbeat, positive music
- Keep volume low (20-30%)
- Don't use copyrighted music (use Descript's library)

### Step 4: Export & Post (5 minutes)

**Export Settings:**
- Format: MP4
- Resolution: 1080p (1920x1080)
- Frame Rate: 30fps
- Quality: High

**Post to:**
- YouTube Shorts (45 seconds)
- Instagram Reels (30 seconds - trim to 30s)
- Facebook Groups (45 seconds)

## 🎯 Content Strategy: What to Include

### Do Include:
✅ **FREE value prop** - Emphasize FREE vs competitors
✅ **Visual demos** - Show app in action (screen recording)
✅ **Social proof** - Testimonials, user count
✅ **Clear CTA** - "Try it FREE - link in bio"
✅ **Problem → Solution** - Show pain point, then solution

### Don't Include:
❌ **Long explanations** - Keep it short (45 seconds)
❌ **Too many features** - Focus on one main benefit (FREE + simplification)
❌ **Complex language** - Use simple, clear words
❌ **Salesy tone** - Be helpful, not pushy

## 📏 Video Length Guidelines

### 45 Seconds (Recommended):
- **Why:** Your best video was 45 seconds
- **Structure:** Hook (3s) → Problem (7s) → Solution (20s) → Proof (10s) → CTA (5s)
- **Platforms:** YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, Facebook

### 30 Seconds (Instagram/TikTok):
- **Why:** Shorter = better for Instagram/TikTok
- **Structure:** Hook (2s) → Solution (20s) → CTA (8s)
- **Platforms:** Instagram Reels, TikTok

### 60 Seconds (LinkedIn/Facebook):
- **Why:** Longer format OK for professional/community platforms
- **Structure:** Hook (5s) → Story (40s) → CTA (15s)
- **Platforms:** LinkedIn, Facebook Groups

**Recommendation:** Create 45-second version first, then trim to 30s for Instagram/TikTok.
