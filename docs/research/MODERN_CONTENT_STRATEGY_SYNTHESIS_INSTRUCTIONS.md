## 🔍 **Synthesis Framework**

### **1. Consensus Analysis**

**Identify where ALL 4 reviewers agree:**
- What strengths do all reviewers acknowledge?
- What concerns do all reviewers share?
- What recommendations appear in all 4 reviews?

**Identify where 3+ reviewers agree:**
- Strong consensus points (likely critical)
- Common recommendations (likely important)

**Identify where reviewers disagree:**
- Contradictory recommendations
- Different risk assessments
- Conflicting priorities

### **2. Critical Blocker Identification**

**What prevents success?** (Mentioned by 2+ reviewers)
- Technical blockers
- Strategic blockers
- Operational blockers

**What are the highest-risk concerns?** (Mentioned by 3+ reviewers)
- Issues that could derail the entire strategy
- Problems that compound if not addressed

### **3. Recommendation Prioritization**

**Categorize recommendations by:**
- **P0 - Critical (Must Fix Before Starting):** Blocks implementation or creates high risk
- **P1 - High Priority (Fix in Pilot Phase):** Important for success, can be addressed during pilot
- **P2 - Medium Priority (Fix During Rollout):** Improves outcomes, can be addressed incrementally
- **P3 - Low Priority (Future Enhancement):** Nice-to-have, can be deferred

**Consider:**
- Impact on user engagement
- Impact on implementation feasibility
- Impact on scalability
- Effort required to implement

### **4. Conflict Resolution**

**Where reviewers disagree, evaluate:**
- Which perspective is more aligned with project goals?
- Which recommendation is more feasible?
- Which addresses the root cause vs. symptoms?
- Can both perspectives be incorporated?

## 🎯 **Specific Questions to Answer**

### **1. Consensus on Critical Issues**

**Question:** What do all 4 reviewers agree MUST be fixed before starting?

**Look for:**
- Length target inconsistency (mentioned by how many?)
- Success criteria realism (mentioned by how many?)
- Pilot testing requirement (mentioned by how many?)
- Automation needs (mentioned by how many?)
- Licensing validation (mentioned by how many?)

### **2. Recommendation Prioritization**

**Question:** Which recommendations have the highest impact-to-effort ratio?

**Consider:**
- Quick wins (low effort, high impact)
- Critical fixes (high effort, but blocks success)
- Nice-to-haves (low impact, can wait)

### **3. Conflict Resolution**

**Question:** Where do reviewers disagree, and which perspective should be followed?

**Examples to look for:**
- Different length recommendations
- Different success criteria thresholds
- Different automation priorities

### **4. Feasibility Assessment**

**Question:** Is the strategy feasible given all concerns raised?

**Consider:**
- Can critical blockers be addressed?
- Is the timeline realistic?
- Are resources sufficient?
- Is the approach scalable?
