## 🎯 **Research Objective**

### **Primary Goal**
Find additional FREE or low-cost modern content sources with:
- **Emotionally powerful stories** that make people cry, laugh, or feel inspired
- **Diverse genres** beyond current curation (self-development, biography, leadership, etc.)
- **ESL-friendly content** (clear language, universal themes, 500-3,500 words)
- **Proven engagement** (high views, shares, or completion rates)
- **Legal accessibility** (public domain, Creative Commons, or easy permission)

### **Target Audience**
1. **ESL Learners** (A2-B2 levels primarily)
   - University students
   - Adult learners
   - Immigrants and refugees
   - International professionals

2. **ESL Programs** (Institutional Customers)
   - University ESL departments
   - Language schools
   - Corporate training programs
   - Non-profit organizations

### **Success Criteria**
Content that makes users:
- ✅ **Complete the story** (70%+ completion rate target)
- ✅ **Feel emotionally moved** (7/10+ emotional impact)
- ✅ **Share with others** ("You have to read this!")
- ✅ **Return for more** (40%+ return rate)
- ✅ **Recommend to programs** (institutional adoption)

## 🔍 **Research Parameters**

### **Content Selection Criteria**

#### **Must Have:**
1. **Emotional Impact** - Stories that make people feel something (joy, inspiration, empathy, determination)
2. **Universal Themes** - Themes that transcend culture and language
3. **Clear Narrative Arc** - Beginning, middle, end (not just facts/data)
4. **ESL-Friendly Length** - 500-3,500 words (2-20 minutes reading)
5. **Accessible Language** - Conversational, not overly technical
6. **Legal Accessibility** - Public domain, CC-licensed, or easy permission

#### **Nice to Have:**
1. **Proven Engagement** - High views, shares, or completion rates
2. **Audio Available** - Original audio recordings (for narration)
3. **Visual Elements** - Images or videos that enhance the story
4. **Multiple Levels** - Content that can be simplified to A1-C2
5. **Series Potential** - Multiple stories from same source/genre

### **Legal Requirements**

#### **Priority 1: Free & No Permission Needed**
- ✅ Public domain content
- ✅ Creative Commons (CC-BY, CC-BY-SA, CC-BY-NC)
- ✅ U.S. Government works (NASA, VOA, etc.)

#### **Priority 2: Easy Permission (Likely Free)**
- ⚠️ Educational use requests (The Moth, Medium authors)
- ⚠️ Non-profit/educational licensing
- ⚠️ Attribution-only requirements

#### **Priority 3: Paid Licensing (Last Resort)**
- ❌ Commercial licensing (This American Life, etc.)
- ❌ Expensive content ($100+ per story)

- **Ease of access** (how easy to get content?)

- **Scalability** (can we get 10+ stories from this source?)

- **5-10 specific stories** to review

Rank sources by:
