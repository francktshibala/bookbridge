## 1. Validated Story List (42 Stories)

### Story Validation Criteria Reference

**Step 0.25 - Source Material Check:**
- ✅ Story-driven (not fact-driven)
- ✅ Length: Can reach 15-45 minutes?
- ✅ Enough content for engaging narrative?

**Step 0.5 - Emotional Impact Validation:**
- ✅ "Text a friend" test: Would someone share this?
- ✅ Clear emotional arc: struggle → perseverance → breakthrough?
- ✅ 5-7 specific emotional moments identified?
- ✅ 3+ ESL resonance multipliers present?
- ✅ Comparison to Helen Keller benchmark

**ESL Resonance Multipliers:**
1. Communication & Language Barriers
2. Learning & Education Journeys
3. Belonging & Identity
