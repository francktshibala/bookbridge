### **NASA Oral Histories**

#### **1. José Hernández - Migrant Farmworker to Astronaut** ⭐ TOP PICK
**Priority:** 🔴 **HIGHEST**  
**Length:** 60+ minutes (perfect for multiple episodes)  
**Emotional Impact:** 10/10  
**ESL Relevance:** 10/10  
**Genre:** Biography, Failure/Comeback, Immigration, Perseverance

**Why This Story:**
- Rejected **11 times** before becoming an astronaut
- Migrant farmworker background (extremely relatable for ESL learners)
- Universal theme: "Never give up on your dreams"
- Perfect length for BookBridge (can be split into episodes)
- Inspiring for immigrants and first-generation students

**Key Themes:**
- Overcoming rejection and failure
- Perseverance against all odds
- Education as pathway to success
- Cultural identity and belonging
- Family sacrifice and support

**Access:** https://historycollection.jsc.nasa.gov/JSCHistoryPortal/history/oral_histories/oral_histories.htm
