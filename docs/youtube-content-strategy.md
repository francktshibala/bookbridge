# BookBridge YouTube Content Strategy

## Overview

**Goal:** Create viral ESL YouTube content that drives users to the BookBridge app.

**Strategy:** Produce two types of content:
1. **Story videos** — Animated/illustrated versions of BookBridge stories with vocabulary and comprehension elements
2. **Viral format videos** — Short vocabulary, quiz, and conversation practice videos using story content

**Target Audience:** ESL learners worldwide (1.5 billion people), primarily A1-B2 levels.

**Channel Name:** English Story Time (@englishstorytime)

**App Link:** bookbridge.app

---

## Video Template (12-15 minutes)

### Structure

| Section | Time | Duration | Content |
|---------|------|----------|---------|
| Hook | 0:00 | 10 sec | Emotional question or statement to grab attention |
| Vocabulary | 0:10 | 2 min | 5-8 key words with visuals, pronunciation, repetition |
| Main Story | 2:00 | 10 min | Illustrated/animated story with text on screen, slow narration |
| Questions | 12:00 | 2 min | 3-4 simple comprehension questions with pause for answers |
| Outro | 14:00 | 30 sec | CTA: app link, subscribe reminder |

### Section Details

**1. Hook (10 seconds)**
- One powerful question or statement
- Examples:
  - "What would you do if you had 24 hours to live?"
  - "My father was my hero — but he couldn't read."
  - "She worked three jobs and never slept."

**2. Vocabulary (2 minutes)**
- Select 5-8 important words from the story
- Format for each word:
  1. Show word on screen with illustration
  2. Pronounce slowly
  3. Say "Repeat after me: [word]" — pause 2 seconds
  4. Show example sentence from story
- Keep visuals simple and clear

**3. Main Story (10 minutes)**
- Use AI animation tools (Animaker, Vidu, DomoAI) or illustrated scenes
- Text ALWAYS visible on screen as characters speak
- Slow, clear narration (use existing BookBridge audio)
- Subtitles match audio exactly

**4. Comprehension Questions (2 minutes)**
- 3-4 questions only
- Keep questions SIMPLE — avoid overwhelming learners
- Question types:
  - Yes/No: "True or False: Sarah was 25 years old."
  - Multiple choice: "Where did the story happen? A) Hospital B) School C) Office"
  - One-word answer: "What is the father's name?"
- Format:
  1. Show question on screen
  2. Pause 5-10 seconds (show countdown or "Think..." text)
  3. Reveal answer
  4. Next question

**5. Outro (30 seconds)**
- "Want to read more? Download BookBridge — link in description."
- "Subscribe for new stories every day."
- Show app screenshot or logo
- End screen with subscribe button

## Visual Style Guide

### Colors (BookBridge Brand)
- Navy: #002147 (primary background)
- Bronze: #CD7F32 (accents, highlights)
- Cream: #F4F1EB (text)
- Dark Brown: #2C1810 (secondary text)

### Typography
- Story text: Georgia, Times New Roman (serif)
- UI/Thumbnails: Bold sans-serif (Bebas Neue, Impact, Anton)

### On-Screen Text Rules
- Font size: Large enough for mobile viewing
- Always white or cream text on dark background
- Subtitles: Bottom of screen, always visible
- Vocabulary words: Center screen, large

## Content Types

### Type 1: Full Story Videos (10-15 min)
- Complete BookBridge story with vocabulary + questions
- Primary content for channel growth
- Drives app downloads

### Type 2: Vocabulary Videos (3-5 min)
- "5 Medical Words from Medical Crisis"
- "10 Words Every Beginner Needs"
- Each word with illustration + pronunciation + example
- Quick, shareable, high replay value

### Type 3: Quiz Shorts (30-60 sec)
- "Can You Understand This?"
- Play audio clip → pause → show question → reveal answer
- Designed for TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts
- Drives traffic to full videos

### Type 4: Conversation Practice (5-10 min)
- Dialogue scenes from stories
- "Listen and Repeat" format
- Slow speech with pauses for practice

## AI Tools for Production

### Animation/Video Generation
| Tool | Use Case | Link |
|------|----------|------|
| Animaker AI | 2D character animation + voiceover | animaker.com |
| Vidu AI | Anime-style video generation | vidu.com |
| DomoAI | Text/image to anime | domoai.app |
| Canva | Thumbnails, simple graphics | canva.com |

### Workflow
1. Extract story text from BookBridge app
2. Select 5-8 vocabulary words
3. Generate illustrations/animations using AI tools
4. Add existing BookBridge audio narration
5. Add text overlays and subtitles
6. Add vocabulary section at beginning
7. Add questions at end
8. Export and upload

## Success Metrics

### Track Weekly
- Views per video
- Watch time (average % viewed)
- Click-through rate (CTR) on thumbnails
- Subscriber growth
- Comments and engagement
- App downloads from YouTube traffic

### Benchmarks
- Good CTR: 4-10%
- Good retention: 40-60% average viewed
- Good engagement: 4%+ (likes + comments / views)
