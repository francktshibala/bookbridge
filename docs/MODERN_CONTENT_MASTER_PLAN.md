## 🎯 **OBJECTIVES & SUCCESS CRITERIA**

### **Primary Goals**
1. **Emotional Impact** - Stories that make people cry, laugh, or feel inspired
2. **ESL Relevance** - Content that resonates with ESL learners and programs
3. **Genre Diversity** - Cover 14 genres (self-development, biography, leadership, etc.)
4. **Legal Accessibility** - Public domain or easy permission
5. **Perfect Length** - 15+ minutes (2,000-3,500 words) for BookBridge benchmark

### **Target Audience**
- **ESL Learners** (A2-B2 levels): University students, adult learners, immigrants, professionals
- **ESL Programs** (Institutional): University departments, language schools, corporate training, non-profits

- ✅ Users complete stories (70%+ completion rate)
- ✅ Users feel emotionally moved (7/10+ impact score)
- ✅ Users share with others ("You have to read this!")
- ✅ Users return for more (40%+ return rate)
- ✅ Programs recommend to students (institutional adoption)
