BookBridge | AI-Powered ESL Reading Platform
BookBridge is an innovative literacy platform designed to bridge the gap between English language proficiency and the world’s greatest literature. By leveraging AI to adapt classic and modern stories into six distinct difficulty levels, BookBridge ensures that every learner can experience the joy of reading at their own pace.
________________________________________
🚀 Key Features
The Reading Experience
•	Adaptive Content: Every title is available in six levels, from A1 (Beginner) to C2 (Proficient).
•	Immersive Audio: High-quality narration with word-by-word highlighting and adjustable playback speeds.
•	Shadowing Mode: Dedicated practice mode to help users improve pronunciation and fluency.
•	Personalized Learning: Integrated ESL dictionary and a spaced-repetition system (SRS) for vocabulary retention.
